Stay Organized with Ease

Our TO-DO application offers a streamlined interface to help you manage your tasks efficiently. Whether it's setting priorities, tracking completed tasks, or planning your day, our app has got you covered.

  • Create and Manage Tasks: Easily add, edit, and organize your tasks.
  • Mark as Important: Highlight crucial tasks to stay focused.
  • Track Completion: Keep track of completed tasks effortlessly.
  • Mood Tracker Integration: Reflect on your day and monitor your emotions over time.

Track Your Mood

Our Mood Tracker allows you to easily log your daily emotions. Select from a range of emojis to express how you're feeling, and add detailed notes to capture your day's experiences. This tool helps you reflect on your emotional well-being over time.

  • Log Emotions: Use emojis to track daily moods.
  • Add Notes: Write detailed reflections

Track Your Reflections

Our Mood Tracker allows you to log daily emotions and provides a weekly view with detailed charts. Monitor your emotional trends over time and save personal notes to reflect on your daily experiences. Whether celebrating great days or acknowledging stress, this feature helps you understand and manage your emotions better.

  • Your Reflections: Monitor your emotions over the week with a detailed chart.
  • Saved Notes: Keep personal notes to reflect on your daily experiences, like "Had a great day!" or "Feeling a bit stressed."
